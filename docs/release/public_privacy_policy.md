# Pet Life — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 May 2026

## Overview

Pet Life is a pet care organizer, diary and local document archive for owners of dogs, cats and other pets.

Pet Life helps users organize pet profiles, reminders, local documents and care-related information.

Pet Life does not provide medical or veterinary diagnosis, triage, prescriptions, treatment recommendations, dosage calculations or emergency guidance.

## Data stored by the app

Pet Life v1.0 stores user-entered data locally on the user’s device.

This may include:

- Pet profiles
- Pet names
- Pet species
- Estimated age
- Breed
- Sex
- Microchip text field
- Veterinarian name text field
- Reminder titles
- Reminder dates and times
- Reminder notes
- Document titles
- Document categories
- Document notes
- Local files selected by the user
- Local file paths used by the app
- Local JSON export files created by the user

## Cloud account

Pet Life v1.0 does not create a cloud account.

Pet Life v1.0 does not provide cloud sync.

Pet Life v1.0 does not upload pet data to a backend server.

## Data sharing

Pet Life v1.0 does not sell personal data.

Pet Life v1.0 does not share user-entered pet data with third parties.

Pet Life v1.0 does not use user-entered data for advertising.

## Analytics and crash reporting

Pet Life v1.0 does not include analytics.

Pet Life v1.0 does not include crash reporting.

If analytics or crash reporting are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy and the relevant store privacy declarations will be updated before release.

## Notifications

Pet Life uses notifications only for reminders and due dates created by the user.

Pet Life v1.0 does not send promotional notifications.

Users can enable or disable notification permissions through system settings.

## Data export

Users can export local data as a JSON file from:

Settings → Data management → Export data

The exported file is created locally on the device.

Users are responsible for how they store, share or delete exported files.

## Data deletion

Users can delete local app data from:

Settings → Data management → Delete local data

This removes locally stored pet profiles, reminders, document metadata and archived local document files from the app storage.

Some exported files outside the app’s managed storage may need to be deleted manually by the user.

## Medical-veterinary disclaimer

Pet Life is an organizer and diary.

Pet Life does not provide:

- Diagnosis
- Triage
- Prescriptions
- Treatment recommendations
- Dosage calculations
- Emergency medical guidance

For concerning symptoms, health questions, medications, dosages or emergencies, users should contact a veterinarian.

## Children

Pet Life is not designed specifically for children.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when app functionality changes.

Material changes should be reflected in the app and store listing before release.
