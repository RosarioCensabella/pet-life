# Pet Life — Screenshot Plan for Google Play v1.0

## Purpose

Create screenshots that clearly show Pet Life as an organizer, diary and document archive for pet owners.

Avoid screenshots that imply diagnosis, triage, prescription, treatment decisions or dosage calculation.

## Google Play preview assets to prepare

Required / recommended assets:

- App icon: 512 x 512 PNG
- Feature graphic: 1024 x 500 PNG or JPG
- Phone screenshots
- Optional tablet screenshots later

The app icon must not include misleading badges, ranking claims, price labels or Google Play promotional text.

## Screenshot content rules

Do:

- Use realistic pet names.
- Show organizer features.
- Show reminders and documents.
- Show privacy/data controls.
- Show disclaimer in at least one screenshot.

Do not:

- Show emergency claims.
- Show diagnosis claims.
- Show “AI veterinarian”.
- Show “treatment recommendation”.
- Show “dosage calculator”.
- Show “replace your vet”.
- Show fake purchase/restore actions.
- Show personal real health data.
