# Pet Life — Terms of Service

Effective date: 18 May 2026

## 1. About Pet Life

Pet Life is a pet care organizer, diary and local document archive.

The app helps users organize pet profiles, reminders, documents and care-related information.

## 2. No veterinary medical advice

Pet Life does not provide medical or veterinary advice.

Pet Life does not provide:

- Diagnosis
- Triage
- Prescriptions
- Treatment recommendations
- Dosage calculations
- Emergency guidance

Pet Life does not replace a veterinarian.

Users should contact a veterinarian for health concerns, symptoms, medication questions, dosages or emergencies.

## 3. User responsibility

Users are responsible for:

- The accuracy of the data they enter
- Keeping their device secure
- Backing up exported files
- Contacting a veterinarian when needed
- Using reminders as organizational tools only

## 4. Reminders

Reminder features are provided for organization.

Pet Life cannot guarantee that notifications will always be delivered, because delivery may depend on device settings, operating system restrictions, battery settings and user permissions.

## 5. Documents

Pet Life can store local document files selected by the user.

Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to store those documents.

Pet Life does not interpret medical records or provide clinical decisions based on documents.

## 6. Data export and deletion

Pet Life provides local JSON export and local data deletion features.

Exported files are controlled by the user after creation.

Deleting local app data may not delete files that the user manually copied or shared outside the app.

## 7. Premium readiness

Pet Life may offer Premium features in the future.

In the current v1.0 build, Premium is informational only and real store purchases are not enabled.

When real subscriptions are enabled, subscription terms, cancellation methods and store billing rules will be updated accordingly.

## 8. Availability

Pet Life is provided as-is.

The developer may update, change or remove features over time.

## 9. Limitation of liability

Pet Life is an organizational tool.

The developer is not responsible for veterinary decisions, missed appointments, missed notifications, incorrect user-entered data or reliance on the app as a substitute for professional veterinary care.
